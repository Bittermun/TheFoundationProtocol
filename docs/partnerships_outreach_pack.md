# Partnerships Outreach Pack

## NGO pitch template

**Problem:** unreliable internet blocks access to education and health content.  
**Solution:** TFP distributes content via broadcast + mesh, offline-first, censorship-resistant.  
**Proof:** local demo node + seeded sample packs + usage logs without PII.

## Community radio pitch template

**Problem:** community stations have weak archival and limited redistribution.  
**Solution:** station content is seeded once, then cached and shared peer-to-peer.  
**Pilot ask:** 3 stations, 90 days, monthly retention + reach metrics.

## Academic/open-access pitch template

**Problem:** research access is uneven in low-connectivity regions.  
**Solution:** package open-access resources into TFP-addressable bundles.

## Required collateral

- 2-minute product walkthrough
- one-page compliance summary
- deployment quickstart
